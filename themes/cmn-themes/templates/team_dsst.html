The Data Science and Sharing Team supports investigators within the NIMH intramural program in creating, distributing, and leveraging large, open datasets to accelerate discovery. We provide tools and training to help the IRP embrace open science and stay on the cutting edge of novel datasets, tools, and platforms. You can find a summary of our recent activities in our Board of Scientific Counsellors report.
